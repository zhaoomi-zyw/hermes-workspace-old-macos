Agent Prompt Templates

Prepared prompts for predictable hands-on moments during the workshop.

1. Interest discovery

I am a German high school student doing a short internship in China. Ask me 5 short questions to discover what kind of AI agent demo would be most interesting for me: school, coding, games, football, China internship, or future career. Then recommend one small project I can build in 25 minutes.

2. China internship diary

Create a small HTML artifact called "My China Internship Diary". It should help me summarize my first impressions, what surprised me, what I learned about work culture, and what AI tools I tried. Use English and German. Keep Chinese notes small.

3. AI study coach

Act as my AI study coach. I am a German high school student. Help me create a 7-day beginner plan to understand AI agents. Each day should have one concept, one practical task, and one reflection question.

4. Career explorer

Act as a career exploration assistant for a German high school student. Explain how AI agents may change 5 future careers. For each career, show what humans will still be good at and how agents can help.

5. Coding buddy

Act as my coding buddy. Help me build a very simple browser game in one HTML file. Keep the code beginner-friendly, explain the main parts, and give me one small improvement task after the first version works.
